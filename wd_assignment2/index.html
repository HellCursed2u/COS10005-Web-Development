About Us

Tica Taco is an Vietnamese chain of taco fast food restaurants which is running through Vietnam which was founded in 2021, Vung Tau city in the South of Vietnam. The restaurants serve many kind of Mexican - famous foods, especially: tacos, burritos, nachos and quesadillas and many more drinks and items. The Tica provides a valuable menu with a suitable price for Vietnamese people, with many size of foods and drinks. The company believe to serve hundreds of customers everyday. In addition, we expected to open more breaches along Vietnam country to serve more customer.
